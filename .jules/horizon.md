# Horizon Journal - Strategic Insights

## 2026-01-17 - "Eat First" Smart Meal Planning
**Insight:** Users have food waste because they forget what's expiring. The current AI meal planner is blind to expiry dates, leading to new ingredients being bought while old ones rot.
**Concept:** **"Eat First" Smart Meal Planning**.
1. **Predictive Lens:** AI suggestions that prioritize expiring ingredients.
2. **Visual Lens:** "Expiring Soon" badges in the pantry list.
3. **Dead End:** Eliminates the manual work of checking dates and searching for recipes that use specific leftovers.

## 2026-01-17 - The Magic Command Center
**Insight:** Users currently have to translate their intent ("I bought coffee") into a UI decision ("Open Transaction Tab -> Enter Amount -> Select Category"). This is cognitive friction.
**Concept:** A "Magic Input" that accepts natural language and automatically routes to the correct action (Transaction, Todo, or Shopping), pre-filling all data. "Don't make me think; just let me say it."
